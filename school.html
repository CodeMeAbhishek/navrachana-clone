Navrachana University, nestled in the vibrant city of Vadodara, Gujarat, offers a rich tapestry of programs that span a wide array of disciplines, fostering an environment where innovation meets education. The School of Business and Law shapes future leaders and legal minds through its Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), integrated BBA-LLB, and advanced degrees like the Master of Business Administration (MBA), Executive MBA, and Master of Laws (LLM). For tech enthusiasts and aspiring engineers, the School of Engineering and Technology provides cutting-edge programs such as Bachelor of Technology (BTech) degrees in Computer Science and Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Electrical and Electronics Engineering, alongside Master of Technology (MTech) degrees in Structural Engineering and Computer Science.

The School of Science caters to inquisitive minds with offerings like the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), BSc in Data Science, an integrated BSc-MSc in Biomedical Science, and Master's degrees in fields such as Chemistry, Life Sciences, Microbiology, and Clinical Embryology. Creativity and design flourish within the School of Environmental Design and Architecture, which presents programs like the Bachelor of Design (BDesign) in Interior Design and Product & Visual Communication, as well as the Bachelor of Architecture (BArch) for those envisioning a future in architecture.

Delving into the humanities, the School of Liberal Studies and Education offers Bachelor of Arts degrees in Journalism and Mass Communication, Humanities and Social Sciences, along with the Bachelor of Education (BEd) and PhD programs across various specializations. Embracing a choice-based curriculum and executive programs, Navrachana University ensures that students receive a holistic education tailored to their passions and career aspirations.
